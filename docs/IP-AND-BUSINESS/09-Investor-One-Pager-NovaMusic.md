## THE SOLUTION: NOVAMUSIC

**Three experiences, one platform:**

**🎵 AI DJ Mode** — Upload your tracks. AI analyzes BPM, musical key, energy, genre, and real-time chart-trending status using GPT-4.1-mini. Generates an optimal setlist with harmonic transitions. Executes automatically. DJ Jeff coaches you throughout.

**🎚 DJ Console** — Professional 4-deck mixer running entirely in the browser via the Web Audio API. Full signal chain: stems emulation, 3-band EQ, filter sweep, delay, reverb, mastering compressor. Camelot Wheel harmonic key matching. Pre-gig AI briefing. No software to install.

**🎉 Party Mode + Crowd Hub** — Share a QR code. The audience joins on their phones. They can: request songs (paid priority = DJ gets more), send emoji reactions (DJ sees crowd energy in real time), vote in DJ battles, tip the DJ, and have their phone turn any color you choose — a synchronized light show.

**🎼 Artist Marketplace** — Independent artists upload tracks, set royalty rates ($0.01–$1.00/play), and earn 85% of every play — direct, with full ISRC play logging for PRO reporting.
